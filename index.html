Megaman Volnutt




Timeline of Megaman Volnutt

  • Born as Megaman Trigger, a Purifier Unit on Elysium
  • Purpose: Eliminate Abberant Units
  • During an attempt to destroy the Master System, was stopped by Sera
  • Victorious, but at the cost of configuration reset (as a new born baby)
  • Memories stored in Data, and sealed away in Nino Island ruins by Yuna
  • Found by Barrell Caskett, and adopted as a grandson
  • Renamed as Megaman Volnutt
  • Finally, sets out to stop Sera from activating the Carbon Reinitialization Program
  • Trapped on Elysium, with the Elder System beginning to reactivate...
